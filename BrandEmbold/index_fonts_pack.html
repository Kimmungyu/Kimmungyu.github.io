Font for embold

By Mungyu Kim / Published in Jan 22. 2019

Requirements

  • Font must can use in both of Digital and Printed Materials
    (Except a Mobile App. I think the best font in Mobile App is a SYSTEM font.)
  • It must have a good visibility and legibility
  • It must have a little unique style to be identified
  • Sans-serif style have a good usability than Serif style, especially in Digital Display *
