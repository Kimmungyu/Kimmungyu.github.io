Summary

EMBOLD For Project Managers

Many IT projects fails, a large number of them because of software problems. Very often this is not transparent to project managers until very late, when they suddenly have to manage a huge delay or increase in costs. One reason is that software development projects cannot be planned and managed like other projects. Another reason is intransparency: Software is a black box, that is hard to grasp and hard to manage. Embold can change this, provide transparency and increase the chance of successful delivery.

Customer Stories

Gain transparency

You don’t know the status of your software? Whether the delivery is on track and the quality high? Embold has helped project managers across different industries to gain full transparency about their software development. Changes in quality become visible immediately and can be addressed before it is too late.

Embold provides intuitive visuals and analytical tools like heat maps and KPIs. This way software quality does not stay a black box but becomes transparent to both developers and project managers. For many customers this has also lead to a significant improvement in communication and mutual understanding.

Deliver software on time

The release date is moving closer but the development progress is not as projected? You fear the software might not be fit for purpose in the end? Embold increases the development velocity throughout the development. When introduced early in projects, it can ensure quality and progress stays on track.

Embold has supported project managers to clearly prioritize software tasks together with their development teams. So when time is running short, everybody is working on the most important aspects first, to ensure the desired quality is released in the end.

Recover Software Projects

Your delivery date is getting closer and closer and your software is still not fully working as required? Over the last nine years we have helped software projects in achieving their target software delivery dates across industries. We come in on short notice, assess the software system down to the source code level and give clear recommendations on how to achieve the desired quality in the available time.

Our Software Recovery projects have proven to be very efficient and successful in development projects of car multimedia systems, Heads-up displays, camera systems, logistics systems, operating systems, telecommunication, core banking, core insurance and many more.

Project Steering + Timeliness

Embold gives you at a glance assessments for software and keeps projects in the right QA orbit and speed.

Timeliness for projects is often a problem, because of imperfect information. Developers and project managers gut-feeling is data-driven. These sub-conscious processes are only as reliable as the information based on, a QA score and an issue radar where to look in the code for problems make assessment faster and more accurate.

Trend analytics that shows real-time how different metrics and KPIs are evolving gives good data if the project runs in the habitable zone, or already left orbit. Enabling to steer projects through hard seas of bad quality without losing speed, due to an appropriate resource allocation in your own projects or at your vendors.

Shift - Left

Everybody gets tired and unfocused over the day, no matter how much coffee we drink. Thus, even the best of us, sometimes make little mistakes or don’t see them. Therefore, we use tools to check on grammar and spelling, even if we know how to write an email without mistakes. It is simply faster to correct it immediately and gives you more time to focus on content.

In development:
Embold has IDE integrations, that continuously check on the quality of development and highlight code issues and anti-patterns while they are written in the IDE by developers. By this a lot of potential Bugs and Issues never even get committed, due to the little hints of Embold.

In review:
EMBOLD AI analysis the code in your commit and compares it with millions of other commits and if similar commits introduced a BUG. EMBOLD shows the reviewer immediately where to check more focussed for a potential bug.

EMBOLD score, shows if the overall quality of the code is deteriorated by the commit. Depending on your project status you can either accept taking technical debt deliberately or reject it.

In project management:
Most projects do not start on the green field, but on an existing code base. EMBOLD scores help you to decide at the start of the project which parts of the code base, you need to be cautious about and which parts you better don’t introduce to your project at all. Especially with microservices, this shifts rework left.

For Website:
Embold is enabling your developers through AI and augmented development to make less rework, and shift these efforts left in the process. Because the later a bug is found, the more costly it will be

Embold helps developers through IDE integration and highlighting of code and design issues to not introduce malicious code in the first place. We help you to keep quality at a decent level with static code analysis, while our AI ensures that bugs are not repeated. Embold is the first tool that enables to shift rework so far left, that more and more rework becomes part of the actual work of the developers. Embold will always safe you money and your developers sleepless nights.

Communication - Support through visualisation and fair KPIs

When it comes to topics like technical debt and its effects, often non-technical persons have a hard time to understand, even if they are responsible for the resources tackling the task in the engine room directly or indirectly. Embold Visuals like our recommendation engine, heat map, trend reports and partitioning plot, let everybody in the meeting fast grasp the concept of the problem at hand. This helps highly to enable the smart thinking people in your company that are not technical to contribute to a solution or at least to understand the issue.

Plus it helps to weld your team together with its managers and bridges the frustrating gap in day to day problem communication. Technical colleagues feel better understood and a more efficient communication is possible.

Since Embold uses KPIs that agregate issues that the developers can link back to a line in the code base, Embold KPIs are actionable for managers as well as developers. When you as a manager say that you need to get the KPI “Accuracy” better throughout your portfolio, because customers complaint about problems with “Accurancy”, then developers know exactly what they must do. Line by line. No misunderstanding, just brilliant execution at a fraction of the time.

This is transparent and fair to everybody who is willing to commit to the process, which builds a team trust as well as goal alignment penetrating different levels of an organisation unseen for most people in their complete work life.
