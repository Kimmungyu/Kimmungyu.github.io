Summary

EMBOLD Solutions – Business

There is a high pressure to innovate, especially in smaller businesses. The time-to-market for new products or features is often very short and at the same time, finding and hiring high qualified developers is increasingly harder. We help our customers to stay ahead of competition and to win the market.

Very often compromises are made in the quality of the developed software, which in turn has an impact on the ability to innovate in the future, since technical debt accumulates. Embold has helped businesses across sectors, countries and markets to overcome this challenge, by enabling high development velocity and high quality at the same time.

Customer Stories

Release software products fast

Embold increases the development velocity throughout the project. When introduced early in the development, it can ensure quality and progress stay on track. Through smart prioritization it ensures time is only spent where it makes a big difference.

Ensure high software quality

For most teams, Embold is part of the Continuous Integration / Delivery pipeline. This way it ensures high software quality standards continuously through the project. In addition, it makes code reviews faster, so they are not skipped when time is short.

Innovate faster

Embold helps to kill many birds with one stone: The productivity of your development teams increases, quality increases, and developers learn to become better engineers at the same time. This helps our customer to prototype and release innovative software faster.

Project Quality Awareness

Embold, helps you to understand the code and architectural quality in your code landscape and projects. It shows at a glance through the Gamma Score, which projects are suffering from high technical debt on code level.

By this you can embolden your developers with better resource allocation and create a positive eustress instead of vicious cycles that burn developers and project milestones. This favors a higher satisfaction of customers and developers.

Shift - Left

Everybody gets tired and unfocused over the day, no matter how much coffee we drink. Thus, even the best of us, sometimes make little mistakes or don’t see them. Therefore, we use tools to check on grammar and spelling, even if we know how to write an email without mistakes. It is simply faster to correct it immediately and gives you more time to focus on content.

Embold is enabling your developers through AI and augmented development to make less rework, and shift these efforts left in the process. Because the later a bug is found, the more costly it will be

Embold helps developers through IDE integration and highlighting of code and design issues to not introduce malicious code in the first place. We help you to keep quality at a decent level with static code analysis, while our AI ensures that bugs are not repeated. Embold is the first tool that enables to shift rework so far left, that more and more rework becomes part of the actual work of the developers. Embold will always safe you money and your developers sleepless nights.

Code Review

Embold provides the ability to do side by side reviews between code changes. Here architects can explain Anti-Patterns to developers and keep the design clean.

Creating a better awareness and mutual understanding, between developers, architects and technical project managers directly at the code level. Saving you time while reviewing, that you can spent more time with creating.
