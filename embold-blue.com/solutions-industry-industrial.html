Summary

EMBOLD For Industrial

Software is becoming core. Many industries have transitioned from human based operation, through extensive use of electronics into an entirely new phase in which software is not only the enabler of all functionality but has also become the one and significantly critical condition for efficient operation.

In many industrial companys, software systems have been in use for decades and are no longer sufficient for the requirements of digitization. In these environments, the current software will be the basis for future innovation. History has shown that core systems are not easy to replace. This is why the current software quality will determine the successful operation and the ability to innovate in the future.

Embedded software engineering

Automative and industrial companies use Embold to improve the KPIs important for embedded software engineering. We embrace KPIs like reliability, usability, efficiency, changeability, portability and many more. Embold enables companies to slice and dice the data needed specific for projects and to facilitate the production of variances.

Embold uses a build-interceptor to analyze all the dependencies of embedded software projects. With this detailed analysis, fine granular metrics and specific design anti-patterns can be identified and linked to KPIs. This helps to get “failure in time” rates to levels unreached at very low costs.

Project Quality Awareness

Embold, helps you to understand the code and architectural quality in your code landscape and projects. It shows at a glance through the Gamma Score, which projects are suffering from high technical debt on code level.

By this you can embolden your developers with better resource allocation and create a positive eustress instead of vicious cycles that burn developers and project milestones. This favors a higher satisfaction of customers and developers.
