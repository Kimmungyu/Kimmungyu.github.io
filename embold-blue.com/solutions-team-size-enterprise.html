Summary

EMBOLD Solutions – Enterprise

Software is becoming core. Many enterprises have transitioned from human based operation, through extensive use of electronics into an entirely new phase in which software is not only the enabler of all functionality but has also became the one and significantly critical condition for efficient operation.

In these environments, the current software will be the basis for future innovation. History has shown that core systems are not easy to replace. This is why the current software quality will determine the successful operation and the ability to innovate in the future.

Customer Stories

Steer your global organization

Embold provides you with all the data needed to steer your software organization, in an intuitive and easy to understand way. It uses heat maps, ratings, charts and built-in KPIs to effectively track crucial aspects of the development like maintainability, reusability, functionality and more.

Innovate faster

Embold helps to kill many birds with one stone: The productivity of your development teams increases, quality increases, and developers learn to become better engineers at the same time. This helps our customer to prototype and release innovative software faster.

Save software costs

We have seen significant cost reduction for many of our customers after introducing Embold. It has helped to both build software that is easier to maintain, and to save costs by increasing the development velocity and decreasing onboarding time for new developers.
