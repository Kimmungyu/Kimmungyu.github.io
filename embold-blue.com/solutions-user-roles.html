Summary

EMBOLD For Executives

Software is becoming core. Many industries have transitioned from human based operation, through extensive use of electronics into an entirely new phase in which software is not only the enabler of all functionality but has also become the one and significantly critical condition for efficient operation. In these environments, the current software will be the basis for future innovation. History has shown that core systems are not easy to replace. This is why the current software quality will determine the successful operation and the ability to innovate in the future.

Customer Stories

Steer your global organization

Embold provides you with all the data needed to steer your software organization, in an intuitive and easy to understand way. It uses heat maps, ratings, charts and built-in KPIs to effectively track crucial aspects of development like maintainability, reusability, functionality and more.

Innovate faster

Embold helps to kill many birds with one stone: The productivity of your development teams increases, quality increases, and developers learn to become better engineers at the same time. This helps our customer to prototype and release innovative software faster.

Save software costs

We have seen significant cost reduction for many of our customers after introducing Embold. It has helped to both build software that is easier to maintain, and to save costs by increasing the development velocity and decreasing onboarding time for new developers.

Shift - Left

Everybody gets tired and unfocused over the day, no matter how much coffee we drink. Thus, even the best of us, sometimes make little mistakes or don’t see them. Therefore, we use tools to check on grammar and spelling, even if we know how to write an email without mistakes. It is simply faster to correct it immediately and gives you more time to focus on content.

Embold is enabling your developers through AI and augmented development to make less rework, and shift these efforts left in the process. Because the later a bug is found, the more costly it will be

Embold helps developers through IDE integration and highlighting of code and design issues to not introduce malicious code in the first place. We help you to keep quality at a decent level with static code analysis, while our AI ensures that bugs are not repeated. Embold is the first tool that enables to shift rework so far left, that more and more rework becomes part of the actual work of the developers. Embold will always safe you money and your developers sleepless nights.

Quality assessments / due dilligince

Embold has its roots in assessing code quality for big corporations. By this the Embold score gives managers a fast overview of repository they have or want to add to their portfolio.

This gives a fast awareness about the overall health of projects, or for rewrite-or-reuse decisions.

Being able to drill down, which KPIs will get impacted. With Embold you can keep your house clean of unhealthy code or to calculate efforts / assets more accurate on the fly.

Quality improvement

As a first to improve the quality of your project the hotspots are identified. Embold takes the developer activities as well as the tickets that effected certain components into account to create a risk metric. The Risk metric combined with a high number of code and design issues and bad metrics marks a certain component as hotspots in Embold.

Together with the development teams, those hotspots can be addressed and a refactoring ticket for the next Sprint can be created. The teams normally prioritize those areas that are or will be under active development in the next sprints. By this your repositories and projects will always have a positive trend and your releases rollout like expected.
