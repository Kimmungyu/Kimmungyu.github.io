Summary

EMBOLD For Other Industry

There is a high pressure to innovate. The time-to-market for new products is often very short and at the same time, finding and hiring high qualified developers is increasingly harder. In many companys, software systems have been in use for decades and are no longer sufficient for the requirements of digitization.

Very often this leads to compromises in the quality of the developed software, which in turn has an impact on the ability to innovate in the future, since technical debt accumulates. Embold has helped many customers across sectors to achieve high development velocity and high quality at the same time.

Customer Stories

Logistic Systems

Embold helped customers from the logistics and industrial sector with their logistics systems. Stability and robustness, as well as maintainability were often of main importance, and Embold helped to increase those quality KPIs.

Ad Delivery and Performance Marketing

Embold analyzed leading ad delivery and performance marketing platforms. By identifying issues from both the design and code level, the quality of these systems has improved. Especially scalability from the software side could be ensured.

Retail Systems

Embold helped software development teams both from online and offline retail in increasing their code quality. Especially when Embold became part of the Continuous Integration and Delivery process significant quality increases became visible.

Legacy System Replacement

Embold helped our customers to replace critical legacy applications that reached the end of their lifetime. It ensures high development velocity and software quality, especially when the timeline, budget and resources are short.

Project Steering + Timeliness

Embold gives you at a glance assessments for software and keeps projects in the right QA orbit and speed.

Timeliness for projects is often a problem, because of imperfect information. Developers and project managers gut-feeling is data-driven. These sub-conscious processes are only as reliable as the information based on, a QA score and an issue radar where to look in the code for problems make assessment faster and more accurate.

Trend analytics that shows real-time how different metrics and KPIs are evolving gives good data if the project runs in the habitable zone, or already left orbit. Enabling to steer projects through hard seas of bad quality without losing speed, due to an appropriate resource allocation in your own projects or at your vendors.

Project Quality Awareness

Embold, helps you to understand the code and architectural quality in your code landscape and projects. It shows at a glance through the Gamma Score, which projects are suffering from high technical debt on code level.

By this you can embolden your developers with better resource allocation and create a positive eustress instead of vicious cycles that burn developers and project milestones. This favors a higher satisfaction of customers and developers.

Quality assessments / due dilligince

Embold has its roots in assessing code quality for big corporations. By this the Embold score gives managers a fast overview of repository they have or want to add to their portfolio.

This gives a fast awareness about the overall health of projects, or for rewrite-or-reuse decisions.

Being able to drill down, which KPIs will get impacted. With Embold you can keep your house clean of unhealthy code or to calculate efforts / assets more accurate on the fly.
