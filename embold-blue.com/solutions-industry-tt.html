Summary

EMBOLD For Technology and Telecom Industry

There is a high pressure to innovate, especially in the technology and telecommunication industry. The time-to-market for new products is often very short or regulatory requirements enforce software changes at short notice. At the same time, finding and hiring high qualified developers is increasingly harder.

Very often this leads to compromises in the quality of the developed software, which in turn has an impact on the ability to innovate in the future, since technical debt accumulates. Embold has helped different customers both in technology and telecommunication with these challenges.

Customer Stories

Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) Systems

Embold helped our customers to identify issues around maintainability, extensibility and software design in their PLM systems. With the recommended changes, the time required to add features or fix bugs decreased significantly.

Real time monitoring systems

Embold helped to identify design issues, code issues and duplication in real time monitoring systems of our clients. This helped to increase the system throughput and reduce downtimes, so that the target systems stay monitored continuously.

SMS Services

Embold helped our telecommunication customers to identify run-time issues in SMS service systems that affected robustness, stability and throughput. Design-level recommendations were derived in order to remove the bottlenecks and achieve desired quality goals.

Legacy System Replacement

Embold helped our customers to replace critical legacy applications that reached the end of their lifetime. It ensures high development velocity and software quality, especially when the timeline, budget and resources are short.

Innovation

Innovation in code is a use case where we as Embold helps you to innovate your code base and architecture.

Project Quality Awareness

Embold, helps you to understand the code and architectural quality in your code landscape and projects. It shows at a glance through the Gamma Score, which projects are suffering from high technical debt on code level.

By this you can embolden your developers with better resource allocation and create a positive eustress instead of vicious cycles that burn developers and project milestones. This favors a higher satisfaction of customers and developers.

Project Steering + Timeliness

Embold gives you at a glance assessments for software and keeps projects in the right QA orbit and speed.

Timeliness for projects is often a problem, because of imperfect information. Developers and project managers gut-feeling is data-driven. These sub-conscious processes are only as reliable as the information based on, a QA score and an issue radar where to look in the code for problems make assessment faster and more accurate.

Trend analytics that shows real-time how different metrics and KPIs are evolving gives good data if the project runs in the habitable zone, or already left orbit. Enabling to steer projects through hard seas of bad quality without losing speed, due to an appropriate resource allocation in your own projects or at your vendors.
